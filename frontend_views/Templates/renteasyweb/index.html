Why Choose RentEasy?

RentEasy is an innovative platform that streamlines rental management for both landlords and tenants. Find properties, pay rent online, request maintenance, and stay in touch — all in one place.

For Tenants

Search properties, pay rent online via M-Pesa, request maintenance, and message your landlord easily.

For Landlords

List properties, manage tenants, track payments and earnings, and handle requests from one dashboard.
